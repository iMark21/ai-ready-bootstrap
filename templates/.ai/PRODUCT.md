# {{PROJECT_NAME}} — Product Vision

> Replace this template with your project's vision. Keep it under 100 lines. The point is the *answer to "why does this exist"*, not the detailed feature list (which lives in `BACKLOG.md` and `specs/PRD.md`).

## Tagline

One sentence that explains what you're building and for whom.

## North Star

What does success look like? List 3-5 concrete things a user (or reviewer, or future contributor) can do once this is shipped.

## Non-Goals

What this project is **not**. Be explicit — most scope debates happen because non-goals were never written down.

## Audience

Who is this for, in order of priority? Be specific (roles, teams, scenarios) — "everyone" is not an audience.

## Tech Stack

**Primary stack:** {{STACK}}

Detail your language(s), frameworks, platforms, and critical dependencies. Link to `ADR-NNN` if there was a choice between alternatives.

## Philosophy

3-5 principles that govern technical decisions. Examples:

- **Specs are the contract.** If code contradicts the spec, update the spec first.
- **Performance budget = N ms for the critical path.**
- **No vendor lock-in below the application layer.**

## Success metrics (optional)

Subjective signals you'll use to know you got it right. Not KPIs unless this is a commercial product.
